Our website is dedicated to educating users about greenwashing, offering insightful resources, practical tips, and real-life examples to help them identify deceptive environmental claims and make informed decision

Our website is committed to raising awareness about greenwashing, equipping users with the knowledge and tools to recognize misleading environmental claims and promote genuine sustainability 

GreenWash Evaluator

Our platform offers a unique 'greenwash score' feature, allowing users to analyze companies' environmental claims and practices to determine their level of authenticity and commitment to sustainability.
